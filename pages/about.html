Our Story

Built by people who couldn't stop moving.

The Sirocco wind doesn't ask permission. Neither do our itineraries.

Not your typical travel agency.

SEROCCO was born from a simple frustration: the gap between dreaming about travel and actually experiencing it. We saw too many people settling for "good enough" because planning felt overwhelming, or because they didn't know what they were missing.

The Sirocco Wind

The Sirocco is a warm, dry wind that originates in the Sahara and crosses the Mediterranean. It doesn't ask permission. It doesn't wait for the right conditions. It simply arrives — transforming everything in its path.

That's exactly how we approach travel planning.

🦩

"Most birds flee the storm. The flamingo walks into it. So do we."
