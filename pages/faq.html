The FORA Partnership

FORA is one of the most respected travel networks in the industry, with decades of experience and relationships with over 4,500 hotels worldwide. As a SEROCCO client, you benefit from everything that partnership provides — preferential treatment at hotels, access to exclusive rates and inclusions, and the kind of behind-the-scenes influence that turns a good trip into an exceptional one.

Preferred partner programs are private networks that hotels reserve for trusted travel advisors — not the general public. Through our FORA partnership, SEROCCO has top-tier access to Four Seasons Preferred Partner, Hilton Impresario, Hyatt Privé, IHG Luxury & Lifestyle, and Virtuoso Travel. What this means practically: room upgrades, early check-in, complimentary breakfast, hotel credits, VIP status, and access to rates not available to the public.

No. These benefits are exclusively available through approved preferred partner advisors. Booking directly with the hotel — even at the same rate — does not include them. This is one of the most concrete reasons to use a travel advisor rather than booking alone.
